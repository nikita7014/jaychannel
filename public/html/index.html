     About JayPad


Enjoy JayChannel Classes from JayPad



JayChannel is a part of HealthyJay-a software ecosystem that offers Providers, Enterprises, caregivers,partners,clients and family the care products including Software Platform (JayPad App for Clients, JayMobile App for Caregivers and FlightDeck Portal for Admin), Device (optional JayPad Tablet) and Content (JayChannel).

Never before, care professionals can shop on the JayChannel Marketplace and participate in their purchased activities virtually and seamlessly from the HealthyJay Platform.

JayChannel Participants host or join the Virtual Classes by using the HealthJay Platform. Participants join from JayPad-the user-friendly tablet App that can be customized based on cognitive levels. Vendors host the classes from our FlightDeck Video Portal. This 3-in-1 Platform brings end-user engagement,enterprise operation and content entertainment together.
